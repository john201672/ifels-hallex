Car Back Seat Rotatable Phone Holder

Want your rear seat passengers to have a relaxing journey in the car?

Car Back Seat Rotatable Phone Holder transforms your phone or tablet into a perfect in-car entertainment system, allowing your passengers to get hands-free entertainment including watching movies, listening to music, reading articles, video calling and so on on the drive.
